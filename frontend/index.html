What is standard deduction?
A standard deduction is a fixed dollar amount that taxpayers can subtract from their adjusted gross income (AGI) to reduce their taxable income. It is typically a simpler alternative to itemizing deductions, where individual expenses like mortgage interest or medical bills must be tracked and proven with receipts.

Old regime vs New regime

Recommendation

Choosing between the old and new tax regimes in India depends on your specific financial situation, primarily your income level and the amount of deductions and exemptions you can claim. The new tax regime is the default option and is generally better for those with minimal deductions, while the old tax regime is better if you have substantial eligible deductions.
